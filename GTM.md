# Go-To-Market

## Positioning

**StackAudit** — "Stripe Atlas for your AI bill." Fast, credible spend audit for seed-stage startups bleeding cash on overlapping AI subscriptions.

## ICP

- Seed / Series A startups (5–50 people)
- Using 3+ AI dev tools
- No dedicated FinOps

## Channels (hypothesis)

1. **Product Hunt** — launch with sample public report
2. **Twitter/X + LinkedIn** — shareable audit links
3. **YC / indie hacker communities** — free audit hook
4. **Outbound** — "free AI stack audit" to portfolio companies

## Monetization (future)

- Free audit + lead capture
- Paid: deeper audit, benchmarks, ongoing monitoring
